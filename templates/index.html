Atomify

From email to Atom

Atomify is an email to Atom gateway. After creating an atomify email address, any email sent to it will be published to your own private Atom feed, which you can subscribe to in the feed reader of your choice, or consume using your own application.

Atomify is PubSubHubbub-enabled! This means that if your consumer - such as Google Reader also supports PubSubHubbub, you will receive new items instantly, as soon as they are posted.
